💻 How to Check Laptops

  • Network: Check the system tray (bottom right) to ensure Wi-Fi and Bluetooth are disabled, or verify Airplane Mode is ON.
  • Recent Files (Windows): Press Win + E to open File Explorer. Click on "Home" or "Quick Access" on the left sidebar to view recently opened files.
  • Recent Files (Mac): Open Finder and click "Recents" on the left sidebar.
  • Word Processor: Have the participant open MS Word or Pages. Ensure it boots to a default blank document and that no background apps (like Grammarly or browsers) are running.

💾 How to Check USB Drives

  • Isolate: ALWAYS plug the participant's USB into your dedicated checking station laptop, never their own.
  • Scan & View: Open File Explorer. Check the "View" tab and ensure "Show Hidden items" is ticked. Review the contents to ensure it is completely empty.
  • Format (If Required): Ask for verbal consent first. Right-click the USB drive in File Explorer, select "Format...", ensure "Quick Format" is checked, and click "Start".
